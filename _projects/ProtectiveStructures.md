# Functions of Main and Front Roll Hoops

## Main Function
- Protect from rollover and impact  
- Prevent cockpit collapse  

## Goals
- Weight reduction  

---

# Function of Anti-Intrusion Plate (AIP)

## Main Function
- Stops objects from entering cockpit (last barrier between car and driver’s feet)  
- Transfers crash loads into rest of the car  

## Goals
- Weight reduction  
- Custom composite plate (spring concept)  
- Serviceability to front bulkhead  
  - Keyhole cutouts for mounting  

---

# Function of Impact Attenuator (IA)

## Main Function
- Absorbs crash energy to reduce force of impact through crumpling  

## Goals
- Weight reduction  

---

# Function of Shoulder Harness

## Main Function
- Acts as a safety restraint for shoulder belts  
- Transfers restraint loads into Main Roll Hoop (MRH)  

## Goals
- Weight reduction  
- Fully rules compliant  

---

# Design Requirements – Roll Hoops

- **F.5.6.3 (RPE):** Minimum 50 mm clearance between driver’s head and RPE  
  - MRH to Braces: 122.428 mm  

- **F.5.9.4:** Main hoop bracing ≤ 160 mm from top point  
  - 137.16 mm  

- **F.5.9.5:** MRH brace angle ≥ 30°  
  - 37.1°  

---

# Design Requirements – Additional

- **F.6.5.2:** Shoulder Harness Bar included angle ≥ 30°  
  - 40°  

- **F.8.5.6.a:** Increased max allowable IA height (150 mm → 175 mm)  
  - 154.9908 mm  

---

# Design Inputs

## Main Roll Hoop (MRH)
- Located inside monocoque (geometry constrained)  
- Must allow accumulator insertion/removal  
- Mounting panels required  
- Mounts placed as far forward as possible (weight savings in ARG25)  

## Front Roll Hoop (FRH)
- Monocoque hump reduced by 0.75 in  
- Laminated into monocoque  
- Bends ≥ 3x outside diameter  

---

# Design Inputs – Additional

## Shoulder Harness
- **T.2.6.1:** Shoulder belt side view angle  
  - Between +10° and −20°  
  - Designed at 5–7° below horizontal  

## Anti-Intrusion Plate
- **F.8.2.1:**  
  - ≥ 1.5 mm steel OR ≥ 4.0 mm aluminum  
- Bolt holes restricted by bulkhead cutout and pucks  

---

# Design: Front Roll Hoop

- Rules clarification allowed removal of bottom ends  
- Dash hump reduction (0.75 in) moved hoop forward and down  
- Geometry shifted to be flush with core  

---

# Design: Main Roll Hoop

- FRH lowered → MRH height increased for protection envelope  
- Mounts placed at panel edge due to accumulator width constraints  

---

# Design: Shoulder Harness

- New part reduces back hump of monocoque  
- Removed area where SH tabs were previously located  
- Height validated with angle constraints  
- Brace angle: 40° (weight optimization)  

---

# Design: Anti-Intrusion Plate

- Keyhole cutouts for accessibility  
- Rules clarification requested for allowance  
- Hole locations positioned close to edge  

---

# Weight Analysis (ARG26 vs ARG25)

## Roll Hoop Assembly

| Component | 2026 | 2025 | Difference |
|-----------|------|------|------------|
| MRH | 7.410 lbs | 7.262 lbs | +0.148 lbs |
| MRH Braces | 2.463 lbs | 1.916 lbs | +0.547 lbs |
| FRH | 4.010 lbs | 4.866 lbs | -0.856 lbs |
| Shoulder Harness Bar & Braces | 1.722 lbs | 2.006 lbs | -0.284 lbs |
| **Total RH Assembly** | 17.751 lbs | 18.153 lbs | **-0.402 lbs** |

## Front Protection

| Component | 2026 | 2025 | Difference |
|-----------|------|------|------------|
| AIP | 3.233 lbs | 3.634 lbs | -0.401 lbs |
| Total Front Protection | 4.708 lbs | 5.261 lbs | -0.553 lbs |

**Total Savings: 0.955 lbs**

# Integration

- Rear wing welded tabs  
- RTML / TSSI tabs welded to top of MRH  
- Steering wheel not compliant at max tilt  
  - Height modification required  
- Head restraint mounts  
- Nosecone mounting to AIP

# Manufacturing & Assembly

## Roll Hoops
- MRH, braces, and SH: welded, bolted to monocoque  
- Manufactured by VR3 Engineering  
- Mounts produced in-house  
- FRH laminated into monocoque  

## AIP
- SendCutSend (aluminum plate)  

## Impact Attenuator
- Standard purchased unit  

## Pucks
- 3D printed
